"Fluffy" Cerberus

Cerberus aka "Fluffy" is a 3 headed dog in Harry Potter.
He is the protector of the Sorcerer's stone or as it was known the Philosopher's Stone.

This is Cerberus. He is the 3 headed dog that protects the Philosopher's Stone in Harry Potter.

This is the Philosopher's Stone that Fluffy was guarding.

In the movie of Harry Potter and the Sorcere's Stone, the only way of getting the stone was to make Fluffy fall asleep by the sound of music. When Harry and the gang went in to get it the harp was already playing and Fluffy was asleep.
