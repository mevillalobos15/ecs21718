Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.

"Civil Rights Leader"

Martin Luther King Jr. was known for his famous "I have a Dream" speech.
He played a vital role in ending segregation.
 King received the Nobel Piece Prize for starting the Civil Rights Act in 1964 and for many other accomplishments.


MLK Biography

  • He advocated for nonviolent protest in the Memphis sanitation worker strike.
  • He provided leadership in the Montgomery bus boycott of 1955.
  • He encouraged and inspired people with his famous "I Have a Dream" speech.
